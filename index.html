Simple Process

How Adoption Works

We've made the adoption process as easy and joyful as possible. Here's how to bring your new best friend home.

1

Browse Pets

Explore dogs, cats, birds, reptiles, and small mammals available for adoption on our website.

2

Pick Your Pet

Find the one that speaks to your heart. Read their story, health info, and personality traits.

3

Apply to Adopt

Fill out a straightforward adoption application on the pet's detail page. It takes just minutes.

4

Get Approved

Our team reviews your application within 1–2 business hours and notifies you by adoption code.

5

Bring Them Home

Complete checkout and arrange delivery or local pickup. Your new family member is on the way!
